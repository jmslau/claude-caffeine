Use cases:

1. Before leaving work, start Claude to do some long-running task. I then commute, and I have my Mac connected to my phone's Wifi sharing before leaving so Claude can continue to work. The Mac is on battery and the lid is closed while I commute. When I get home, it's done. I want my laptop to go to sleep when Claude goes idle.
2. On the weekend, before I go out, I start Claude to do some work. It finished pretty quickly. Later in the day, I use my phone to use Claude Remote to continue to work with it via my phone. Computer stays home all day. It might be plugged into the power. It might be plugged into an external display. I don't want my Mac to go to sleep when Claude goes idle.
3. I have my laptop closed in my backpack, and Claude is running on it. I am. using Claude Remote to continue to work with it via my phone. When it goes idle, I don't want my Mac to go to sleep.
